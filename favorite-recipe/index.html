Raspberry Jam Waffles

A wonderful waffle with a topping of raspberry jam, fresh raspberries, and topped with powdered sugar!

Ingredients

  • 2¼ cups flour
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • 2 cups milk
  • ½ cup butter(melted)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Your favorite raspberry jam to taste
  • Fresh raspberries to taste
  • Powdered sugar to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat a waffle iron
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. In another mixing bowl, whisk milk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla. Combine the two mixtures together to make a batter.
  3. Add batter to waffle iron and cook until golden.
  4. Carfully remove waffles from iron as iron will be hot. Set aside until they are ready to be served.
  5. Top waffles with raspberry jam and fresh raspberries.
  6. Sift powdered sugar on top of waffle and serve.
